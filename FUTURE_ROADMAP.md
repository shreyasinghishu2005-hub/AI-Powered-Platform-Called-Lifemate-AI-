# LifeMate AI Future Roadmap

## 0-3 Months: Hackathon to MVP

Product:

- Mobile prototype
- Voice assistant
- Medicine reminders
- Emergency contacts and SOS demo
- Government scheme guidance prototype
- Document summarizer
- Translation
- Family dashboard prototype

Technology:

- OpenAI/Gemini provider adapter
- PostgreSQL schema
- pgvector knowledge base
- Offline SQLite reminders
- Basic safety classifier

Languages:

- English
- Hindi or one local launch language
- One additional regional language

## 3-6 Months: Pilot Readiness

Product:

- Real family invites
- Reminder adherence tracking
- Budget planner
- Career guidance roadmap
- Wellness check-ins
- Saved offline guidance
- Push notifications

Technology:

- Production auth
- Notification service
- Document OCR pipeline
- RAG admin tooling
- Analytics dashboard
- Consent management

Pilots:

- One clinic/pharmacy partner
- One school/university partner
- One NGO/rural community partner

## 6-12 Months: Regional Expansion

Product:

- More language packs
- WhatsApp/SMS companion
- Institution dashboard
- Field worker mode
- Voice translation
- Caregiver summaries

Technology:

- Provider failover
- Cost-based model routing
- Advanced offline sync
- Audit logs
- Security review
- Compliance framework

Business:

- Family subscription launch
- Institutional pilots converted to paid contracts
- Government scheme data partnerships

## 12-18 Months: Trust and Intelligence

Product:

- Personalized long-term memory with consent
- Doctor appointment preparation
- Form filling guidance
- Resume builder
- Interview voice practice
- Community resource referrals

Technology:

- Evaluation suite for AI safety and accuracy
- Multilingual quality benchmarks
- RAG freshness monitoring
- Human review workflows
- Tenant isolation for institutions

## 18-24 Months: Platform Scale

Product:

- Regional marketplaces for trusted services
- Public API for partners
- Family health and wellbeing trends
- Smart device integrations
- Low-cost feature phone flows

Technology:

- Multi-region deployment
- Data residency controls
- Advanced personalization engine
- On-device small model features
- Enterprise integration APIs

Business:

- Government deployments
- Telecom bundles
- Employer wellness plans
- API licensing
- International expansion

## Long-Term Vision

LifeMate AI becomes a trusted life operating system for everyday people:

- It understands your language.
- It remembers what matters with permission.
- It works even when the internet is weak.
- It protects your privacy.
- It connects family and institutions only when you choose.
- It helps complete real tasks, not just generate text.
