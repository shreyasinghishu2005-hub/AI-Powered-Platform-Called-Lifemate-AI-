# LifeMate AI Pitch Deck

## Slide 1: Title

LifeMate AI

Multilingual AI companion for health, safety, money, documents, careers, and everyday life.

Tagline:

Your life assistant, in your language, online or offline.

## Slide 2: Problem

Everyday life is too complex for millions of people.

- Elderly users miss medicines and need emergency support.
- Rural users struggle with government schemes and documents.
- Students need career guidance and mental wellness support.
- Working professionals need help managing time, money, and information.
- Most tools assume English, literacy, stable internet, and digital confidence.

## Slide 3: Insight

The next billion AI users will not start with prompts.

They will start with voice, local language, trust, and urgent daily needs.

## Slide 4: Solution

LifeMate AI is a voice-first multilingual personal AI companion.

It helps users:

- Set medicine and health reminders
- Understand government schemes
- Send emergency SOS
- Plan budgets
- Summarize and translate documents
- Get career guidance
- Check in on mental wellness
- Plan each day
- Share important updates with family

## Slide 5: Product Demo

Demo flow:

1. User speaks in local language.
2. LifeMate creates a medicine reminder.
3. User asks about a government scheme.
4. LifeMate asks eligibility questions.
5. User uploads a document.
6. LifeMate summarizes and translates it.
7. SOS alert updates family dashboard.
8. Offline mode keeps reminders working.
